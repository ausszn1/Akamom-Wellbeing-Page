Letter From The Commitee

About The Commitee

Dear Aga Khan Academy Mombasa Students, In times of darkness, it's crucial to remember that you are not alone. The wellbeing committee is here to remind you of the light within you, even when the shadows of depression may seem overwhelming. Our motto, "No darkness is too vast for the light within to illuminate. Seek support, seek hope, seek healing," encapsulates our commitment to supporting your mental health journey. We understand that navigating through depression can be challenging, especially as students juggling academic pressures and personal growth. That's why we invite you to join our community platform dedicated to mental health support. This is a safe space where you can share your stories, find guidance, and spread love within the AKAMOM community.Remember, reaching out for help is a sign of strength, not weakness. Together, we can illuminate even the darkest corners of our minds and foster a culture of understanding and support. You are valued, you are loved, and you are not alone.

Sincerely

The Wellbeing Commitee
